Fade Me Out

CONNECTIONS BEYOND CONTACT

The stigma associated with online friendship, that “real” intimacy can only be created via physical encounter, has not faded.

There are a multitude of people from different countries, different upbringings, different walks of life who have real friendships. Connections that could only be possible through the internet.
